## ✅ Factual Questions (Answered)

### Q1: What is the expense ratio of Groww Large Cap Fund?
**A:** Direct Plan: ~0.35% p.a. | Regular Plan: ~1.05% p.a. (Total Expense Ratio as per latest factsheet). Direct plans have lower TER since no distributor commission is included.  
📎 **Source:** https://groww.in/mutual-funds/groww-large-cap-fund-direct-plan-growth

---

### Q2: What is the ELSS lock-in period for Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund?
**A:** The mandatory lock-in period for all ELSS funds is 3 years (36 months) from the date of allotment, as mandated by SEBI. Each SIP instalment has its own 3-year lock-in from its individual allotment date. There is no exit load on ELSS funds.  
📎 **Source:** https://www.sebi.gov.in/legal/circulars/jul-2013/circular-on-equity-linked-savings-scheme_25921.html

---

### Q3: What is the minimum SIP amount for Groww Flexi Cap Fund?
**A:** The minimum SIP amount for Groww Flexi Cap Fund is ₹100 per month with no upper limit. You can set up weekly, monthly, or quarterly SIPs via Groww.  
📎 **Source:** https://groww.in/mutual-funds/groww-flexi-cap-fund-direct-plan-growth

### Q4: How do I download my capital gains statement on Groww?
**A:** On Groww, go to Profile → Reports → Capital Gains Report, select the financial year, and download as PDF or Excel. For a consolidated statement across all AMCs, use CAMS (mycams.com) or KFintech (kfintech.com).  
📎 **Source:** https://groww.in/blog/how-to-download-capital-gains-statement-in-groww

---

### Q5: What is the exit load for Groww Liquid Fund?
**A:** Groww Liquid Fund follows the SEBI-mandated graded exit load: 0.0070% on Day 1, tapering to 0.0045% on Day 6, and Nil from Day 7 onwards. This applies as per SEBI's 2019 circular on liquid fund exit loads.  
📎 **Source:** https://www.sebi.gov.in/legal/circulars/sep-2019/circular-on-exit-load-for-liquid-mutual-fund-schemes_44381.html

---

### Q6: What is the riskometer rating for Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund?
**A:** Riskometer: Very High Risk. ELSS funds are diversified equity funds investing primarily in equities, which carry high market risk. The riskometer is updated monthly based on portfolio composition as per SEBI guidelines.  
📎 **Source:** https://www.sebi.gov.in/legal/circulars/oct-2020/product-labeling-in-mutual-funds_47775.html

---

### Q7: What is the benchmark index for Groww Flexi Cap Fund?
**A:** The benchmark index for Groww Flexi Cap Fund is the Nifty 500 TRI (Total Return Index). Performance of the fund is measured against this benchmark.  
📎 **Source:** https://groww.in/mutual-funds/groww-flexi-cap-fund-direct-plan-growth

---

### Q8: Does Groww ELSS offer tax benefits under Section 80C?
**A:** Yes. Investments up to ₹1.5 lakh per financial year in ELSS funds qualify for deduction under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961. This makes ELSS the only equity mutual fund category with a tax deduction benefit.  
📎 **Source:** https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/knowledge-center
